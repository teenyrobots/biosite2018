Hi there! I am an experience designer with a focus in journalism and education. I design digital products, games, comics, and more to help folks tell better stories.

I cofounded Symbolia, was a Knight Fellow at the American University Game Lab, and I teach digital comics in the CCA Comics MFA program. I've worked with clients like Vox Media, PBS, Bed Bath & Beyond, Showtime, and more.

My work has been written about by Newsweek, Fast Company, and Citylab. I have spoken about media innovation at SXSW, Game Developers Conference, and on The Verge's Top Shelf. Have a look at my full CV here, or book me to speak at your event.
